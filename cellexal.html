CellexalVR

What it is.

CellexalVR is a research project at the Computational Genomics Lab at the Division of Molecular Hematology and Stem Cell Center project that I am glad to be a part of.

More precisely, CellexalVR is a virtual reality platform for visulisation of single-cell gene expression data. If you want to know more, please check out the website here,
